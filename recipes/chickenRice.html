Chicken and Rice

Description

Quick and easy meal

Good for meal preps or last minute dinner

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • White Rice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Pre heat pan
  2. Add table spoon of olive oil
  3. Dice chicken breast and add to pan, cook until juices are clear and there is no pink flesh
  4. Add cup of rice to a pan
  5. Cover rice with water and bring to boil
  6. Set timer for 15 minutes
  7. When timer is up and chicken is cooked plate up the chicken and rice
  8. season with salt and pepper
