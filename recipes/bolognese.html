Bolognese

Description

Traditional pasta dish referred to as Raggu in Italy

Usually made with beef in a tomato based sauce

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Two tins of chopped Italian tomatoes
  • Tomato puree
  • 1 onion
  • Olive oil
  • Mushrooms
  • Italian herbs
  • 3 table spoons of Port

Instructions

  1. Pre heat pan
  2. Add table spoon of olive oil
  3. Chop onions and add to pan
  4. Sweat off onions until soft
  5. Add minced beef
  6. Brown minced beef
  7. Chop mushrooms and add to pan
  8. After 5 minutes add two tins of chopped tomatoes and stir
  9. Season with Italian herbs
  10. Add 3 table spoons of Port
  11. Leave to cook down for 20 minutes to half an hour
